# 🥗 CaloAI — Project Overview

## 🎯 The Mission
**CaloAI** (formerly NutriTrack) is a high-performance, AI-driven nutrition and digital vitality platform. Our goal is to bridge the gap between clinical nutrition research and consumer technology, providing users with an "unfair advantage" in managing their health.

We provide precision tracking, AI-powered food recognition, and direct access to board-certified nutritionists to help users translate biometric data into actionable life changes.

---

## 👥 Target Audience

### 1. Patients / Clients
The primary users of the platform. They use CaloAI to:
- **Scan Meals**: Instant caloric and macro-nutrient breakdown via AI.
- **Track Progress**: Monitor weight, metabolic health, and nutritional adherence.
- **Consult**: Work 1-on-1 with nutritionists to receive personalized diet plans.

### 2. Nutritionists
The "For Professionals" side of the app. They use the platform to:
- **Manage Clients**: View patient logs, progress charts, and health data.
- **Provide Care**: Upload diet plans, schedule consultations, and log session notes.
- **Apply to Join**: A dedicated application flow for specialists to join the platform.

### 3. Administrators
The internal team managing the ecosystem:
- **Approve Professionals**: Review and accept nutritionist applications.
- **System Stats**: Monitor platform growth and user engagement.
- **User Management**: Handle account status and permissions.

---

## 💡 Core Philosophy
- **Precision**: Moving beyond generic tracking to data-driven insights.
- **Aesthetics**: A premium, "luxury watch" feel for health software.
- **Security**: Medical-grade encryption for sensitive biometric data.
- **Intelligence**: Leveraging computer vision to eliminate manual logging friction.
