# ✨ Features Deep Dive

CaloAI's value is in its specialized features that simplify nutrition for users and clients.

---

## 🍽️ AI Food Recognition (The "Scanner")

The standout feature of CaloAI.
-   **Functionality**:
    -   Point-and-shoot calorie estimation.
    -   Instant macro-nutrient breakdown (Protein, Carbs, Fats).
    -   Volume and density analysis (clinical grade).
-   **User Flow**:
    1.  User opens `AI Scan` from the sidebar.
    2.  User takes a photo or describes a meal.
    3.  System returns a card with the name, kcal, and macro breakdown.
    4.  User saves the entry to their `Meal Log`.

---

## 📈 Individual Progress Tracking

Traditional tracking revamped for precision:
-   **Daily Calories**: Visual charts showing daily intake vs. weight goals.
-   **Weight History**: A timeline of body composition changes.
-   **Nutrient Trends**: Long-term tracking of specific macro-nutrient adherence.

---

## 💼 Nutritionist-Client Collaboration

CaloAI facilitates a professional relationship:
-   **Professional Onboarding**: Nutritionists apply via a dedicated page, providing their specialty and resume.
-   **Assigning Specialists**: Admins assign clients to nutritionists.
-   **Diet Plans**: Nutritionists can upload and manage diet plans for their assigned clients.
-   **Consultation Notes**: A secure space for session history and clinical observations.

---

## 🔐 Privacy & Biometric Data

-   **Encryption**: All user data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
-   **Anonymity**: Users can opt to share only necessary biometric data with their assigned professional.
-   **Data Ownership**: Users can export or delete their history at any time.
